Utilities 

New Customer Information

You must have these things before city services are connected.

Valid Mailing Address

Valid drivers Lisences or Photo ID (US)

Lease Agreement or Proof of Ownership

Fees

City Utility Deposit $150.00

Connect Fee $35.00

Payment Information

Payments Due: 15th of month by 5PM or 10% late charges apply.

Last day to pay 25th by 5PM - disconnect fees apply & disconnects on 26th @ 8AM.

Methods of Payment: Bank draft, mail, cash, check or money order. (only checks in drop box please)

Checks by phone. (please allow 7 days for processing)

Credit card by phone, debit/credit card by phone, or online. (please allow 3 days for processing)

Trash & Recycling

Trash Pick-Up Thursday mornings. Trash containers must be out to the curb by 7AM.

Recycle bins pick up every other Thursday morning & must be at curb by 7AM.

Pets & Animals

The City of Granger Leash Law - Requires all pet owners to show proof of rabies vaccination and to purchase a City License Tag for each pet in the household.

If you have any questions, please contact our City Office at (512) 859-2755.

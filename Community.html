Welcome to Our Community

Our friendly community is comprised of approximately 1500 of the friendliest citizens of Texas and situated along Highway 95, just north of Taylor, Texas. We are approximately 18 miles east of Georgetown, and 1 hour from Austin. Our location provides a vital gateway to the City of Austin, Texas. 
 
Located just minutes from the City of Taylor, Granger provides access to all of the ammentites of the big city with all of the small town feel. Additionally, our community offers professional city services to include a well trained police force and volunteer fire department that continues to keep our city safe.
 
Our local merchants compliment the diversity in the area through a variety of establishments ranging from small gift shops and restaurants. In addition, the City of Granger is proud to be the home of the Granger Independent School District, which provides a healthy learning environment for our children as signified through their quality ratings.

The Court Clerk serves as the administrative side of the Municipal Court. The responsibilities of the Court Clerk include collecting assessed fines and fees, docketing cases for efficient adjudication, processing filed complaints, on time and accurately and responding courteously to requests for information from the public. The Municipal Court provides all persons the right to trial by judge or jury and follows the procedures set forth under Texas law.


If you were issued a citation, the appearance date is the deadline date to pay for your fine, set a court date, or make other arrangements. If you have been released on bond, your appearance date will be set once you have contacted the Court Clerk at (512)859-2755 EXT- 404 or e-mail court@cityofgranger.org.  Communication to the court may also be done by mail.


Correspondence about citations should be made to court@cityofgranger.org, if possible. Phone calls to the court will be returned as time permits.

Hours are Monday thru Friday from 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. Out for lunch from 1:00 p.m. - 2:00 p.m.
